General help

Questions about how the app works, features, or troubleshooting? Email or message us with a short description of what you’re trying to do and what you see on screen.

Account or sign-in issues

Problems signing in with Apple, Google, or email? Tell us which option you use and any error message you see. We’ll help you get back into your account.

Rewards, restaurants & subscriptions

For points, visits, redemptions, a specific restaurant, or Comeleal Pro billing: include your account email (if possible), restaurant name, and approximate date. Subscription changes on iOS are also managed in your Apple ID settings.

Privacy-related questions

For how we handle personal data, read our Privacy Policy. You can still email us at support@comeleal.com for privacy requests.

FAQ

  • How do I earn points? Visit participating restaurants and follow in-app instructions (for example, showing your QR code to staff where applicable).
  • My points or visit didn’t show up. Contact support with the restaurant name and date of your visit so we can look into it.
  • I can’t sign in. Try updating the app, check your internet connection, and use the same sign-in method you used when you registered. If it still fails, email us.
  • How do I cancel a subscription? On iPhone, open Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, select Comeleal, and cancel there. We can help if something looks wrong in the app.
  • Where are your legal terms? See Terms of Use and Privacy Policy below.

Preguntas frecuentes

  • ¿Cómo gano puntos? Visita restaurantes participantes y sigue las indicaciones en la app (por ejemplo, mostrar tu código QR al personal cuando aplique).
  • No veo mis puntos o visita. Escríbenos con el nombre del restaurante y la fecha de la visita para revisarlo.
  • No puedo iniciar sesión. Actualiza la app, revisa tu conexión y usa el mismo método con el que te registraste. Si sigue fallando, contáctanos por correo.
  • ¿Cómo cancelo una suscripción? En iPhone: Ajustes → Apple ID → Suscripciones, elige Comeleal y cancela ahí. Si algo se ve mal en la app, podemos orientarte.
  • ¿Dónde están los términos legales? Consulta los Términos de Uso y la Política de Privacidad más abajo.
