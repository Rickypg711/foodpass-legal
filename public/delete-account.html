Comeleal Account Deletion

You can ask us to delete your Comeleal account and the personal data associated with it. This page explains how to submit a request and what to expect.

How to request account deletion

Send an email from the address you use for Comeleal (or, if you no longer have access to it, explain that in your message).

Email: paredesricardog@gmail.com

What to include in your email

  • The email address tied to your Comeleal account (your “account email”).
  • A clear statement that you want your account deleted (for example: “Please delete my Comeleal account and associated data”).

Including this information helps us verify and process your request promptly.

What data will be deleted

After we confirm your request, we will delete or anonymize personal data associated with your account, including for example:

  • Your profile and account information linked to Comeleal.
  • App activity and preferences stored with your account, where applicable.

The exact categories may depend on how you use Comeleal; we will delete what we hold in a way that identifies you as a user, unless we must keep limited information as described below.

Data we may retain

Some information may be kept where the law requires it or where we have a legitimate need to retain it for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance. When we retain data for these reasons, we limit use to those purposes and keep it only as long as necessary.

When deletion happens

We aim to complete account deletion within 7 days after we verify your request. We may contact you if we need additional information to confirm your identity or your account.
